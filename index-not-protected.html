Travel Logistics

Lavacchio is 30km (hell yeah, metric system baby!) east of the beautiful city of Florence. Please plan on coordinating your own travel to the estate as our guests will be arriving at various days and times.

Flying In

The closest major airport is Florence (Amerigo Vespucci), where you should be able to arrange transportation (~45min car ride) to Lavacchio.

Other Nearby Major Cities

The other major airports to get in & out of are Rome (1.5 hour train south) and Milan (2 hour train north). We suggest getting a train from these cities to Florence then arranging a ride from the Firenze train station to Lavacchio. We will provide contact information for a driving service at a future date.

Rental Cars

We are excited to host all of the events for the wedding on property, so a rental car is not necessary. If you plan to rent a car, please let us know & be careful on the narrow roads! Parking is available for a limited number of vehicles.

Property Information & Rules

Fattoria Lavacchio is an incredible certified Organic Winery making some of the best Chianti in all of Italy! But that means it comes with some rules...

Italians take the organic label very seriously! So unfortunately, public consumption of non-certified Italian organic products on the property is not allowed. So no Peronis and no Diet Coke. Sorry! However, Lavacchio will provide organic alternatives available to be charged to your rooms if you need a snack or soda. Please don't break the rules on this! If you bring outside products, please ensure they stay in your bedroom.

You can read more about Lavacchio's sustainability principles and story here.

Villas

Lavacchio consists of 4 separate villas, each with their own character (and 2 pools, no big deal). We have space for everyone to stay on property with us. No need to make any reservations - we'll provide room assignments at a later date.

We are happy to cover the cost of your room as a thank you for traveling all this way to celebrate with us! Even if you can only make it for a couple days, we'd still love to see you!

What you are responsible for

We will also be covering costs for breakfast each day, dinner the evening before the wedding, food and drink for the wedding/reception, and a meal the day after the wedding.

There will be some meals and activities you may wish to enjoy outside what we cover. You are able to charge any additional costs (meals, drinks, snacks, activities) to your room and settle up with Lavacchio at the end of your stay. Reminder - please don't consume outside food/beverage outside of your room unless purchased on property.
